My Third Blog Post: Meat recipes - Baked pork ribs in BBQ sauce.

August 20, 2023

By Dean Dolic

Here's a simple recipe for pork ribs in BBQ sauce:

Ingredients:

  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce (you can buy pre-made or make it at home)
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of mustard
  • 2 tablespoons of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon of onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika

    • Instructions:

    1. Heat the oven to 180 Â°C.
    2. Wash the pork ribs and dry them with paper towels. Remove excess fat if necessary.
    3. Salt and pepper the ribs to taste.
    4. In a bowl, mix the BBQ sauce, brown sugar, mustard, apple cider vinegar, garlic powder, onion powder and smoked paprika.
    5. Place the pork ribs in a large roasting pan or on a wire rack set over a roasting pan. Bake in a preheated oven for 1 hour.
    6. After one hour, brush the ribs with BBQ sauce and return them to the oven.
    7. Bake for another 30-45 minutes, occasionally basting the ribs with additional BBQ sauce. The fish should be soft and fall away from the bones.
    8. When the ribs are cooked, remove them from the oven and cover them with foil. Let them rest for 5-10 minutes.
    9. Cut the ribs into individual pieces and serve with the remaining BBQ sauce.

    This recipe will allow you to enjoy juicy and tasty pork ribs soaked in delicious BBQ sauce. Serve them with favorite side dishes such as baked potatoes or vegetable salad. Bon Appetit!
